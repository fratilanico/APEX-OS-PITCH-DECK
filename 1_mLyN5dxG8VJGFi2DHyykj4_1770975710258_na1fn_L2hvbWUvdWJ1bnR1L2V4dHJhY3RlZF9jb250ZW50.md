# APEX OS - Business Information

## Value Proposition
APEX OS transforms 32,000 proven InfoAcademy customers into a zero-CAC distribution network, enabling builders to go from idea to revenue in 30 days. It gives equity to builders instead of taking it, positioning itself as the operating system for the AI-first economy.

## Pricing & Revenue Model
The revenue model is based on subscriptions, with projected Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) and Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR). The Lifetime Value (LTV) of a customer is calculated based on different churn scenarios, indicating a recurring revenue stream.

- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $50B

# APEX OS Business Information

## Value Proposition
APEX OS transforms 32,000 proven InfoAcademy customers into a zero-CAC distribution network, enabling builders to go from idea to revenue in 30 days. It is an operating system for the AI-first economy that focuses on building and launching products rather than traditional teaching or accelerating.

## Pricing and Revenue Model
The company projects an ARPU of $149/month per builder. The revenue model is a subscription-based service with a 70/30 revenue share with the builders, where builders keep 70% of the revenue they generate.

## Market Sizing
- **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** $420B (Online education + No-code + AI development)
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM):** $50B
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM):** $5M (Year 1) -> $50M (Year 3) -> $250M (Year 5)

## Competitive Advantages and Moats
- **Zero Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Leveraging an internal distribution network of 32,000 paying customers from InfoAcademy.
- **High Conversion Rate:** 3-5x higher than industry average due to a warm audience.
- **Speed to Market:** Enables builders to go from idea to revenue in 30 days.
- **High Gross Margins:** 85% gross margins due to AI-native infrastructure.
- **Builder-Centric Equity Model:** APEX OS gives 5% of its equity to builders, creating strong alignment and a powerful incentive structure.
